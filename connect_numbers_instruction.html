⬅ Home 🔗 Connect Numbers

📖 How to Play

🎯 Goal

Merge numbers together until you reach the target number (e.g., 2048). The target will increase as you progress.

🕹️ Controls

  • Click tiles to highlight connected numbers of the same value.
  • You must select at least 2 tiles to combine.
  • Press Space or click the Combine button to merge.

⚡ Game Rules

  • The merged number is placed on the last tile selected.
  • After merging, tiles fall down (gravity) and new numbers appear.
  • The lowest number will increase as you reach higher tiles.
  • If all tiles are too small, they will be removed automatically.

💾 Save & Load

  • Click 💾 Save State to copy a code for your current game.
  • Click 📂 Load State and paste the code to continue later.

🎉 Tip

Plan your moves ahead! Combining more tiles at once gives bigger rewards.
